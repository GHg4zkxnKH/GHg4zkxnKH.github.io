DO DOGS FEEL COLD?

While all dogs are susceptible to cold, puppies, smaller dogs, dogs with short hair and senior dogs will feel the cold more sharply and are more likely to suffer adverse effects from it.

It's important to keep them warm when the temperature drops, especially when taking them out on a walk when they are more exposed to the elements.

What makes our sweaters special?

NATURAL MATERIALS

Dogs are commonly susceptible to allergies. If your dog is itching and sneezing, an allergic reaction may be responsible.

Synthetic fibers require certain chemicals during manufacturing. These chemicals are known to be allergenic and allow the accumulation of bacteria, mold, mildew and dust mites that can cause skin problems.

Our sweaters are made of 100 percent cotton that is naturally hypoallergenic.

STRETCHY & WARM

By using special knitting techniques, we are able to add extreme elasticity to natural fabrics without the need for using synthetics.

Our stretchy fabric fits small to large chests, wrapping around your dog like a second skin with comfort.

Tight rib stitching holds heat in its layers, keeping your dog warm and cozy.

EASY TO CARE

Our sweaters are machine-washable, easy to clean and keep free of allergens.

Durable, high tensile strength fabric keeps the colors vibrant.

CERTIFIED SAFETY

STANDARD 100 by OEKO-TEX® is one of the world's best-known labels for textiles tested for harmful substances. It stands for customer confidence and high product safety.

Our fabric materials are tested by independent test institutes and certified to meet human-ecological requirements for baby clothing.
