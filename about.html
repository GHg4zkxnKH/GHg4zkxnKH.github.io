About Us


Puppy Cozy is the home of the premium knitwear sweaters that dogs love.
We design and produce high quality, simple, functional and comfortable dog sweaters. Every piece is meticulously crafted with our own production of 100% cotton premium fabric.

As noticed by many owners, dogs build a connection with our sweaters and insist keeping them on! We think it has something to do with the softness of our fabric and the way it wraps around them with comfort, making them feel like you are holding them even when you aren't.
